Hi Everyone! This Society Central category houses all the different society channels for those of you interested in joining and participating in any of the activities mentioned below. For you to become a member of any society please message an admin or moderator to relay your interest and you will be added to that society role giving you full access to that societies activities. These society channels were created with the hopes of allowing people to find a place to collaborate and expand on their creative interests in manners that may have not been accessible otherwise. Please respect all the rules mentioned in the welcome channel and have a nice time.

Hey @everyone We're officially launching our societies feature for the server. The societies currently on offer include: The Art Society The Music Society The Cinema Society The Photography Society The Culinary Society The Travel Society The Anime Society The Tech Enthusiasts Society The Memes Society The Environmental Society The Writing Society The Entrepreneurs Society The Sports Society If you're interested in joining any society or becoming the President of any particular one, please feel free to post your interest in the Society Applications Channel and @Moderator 1 in your message so that we can add you to the respective society.
