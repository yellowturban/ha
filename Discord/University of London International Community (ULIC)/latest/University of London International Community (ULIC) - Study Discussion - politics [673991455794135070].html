@Romana 🇸🇰 generally, 200 courses require more research and analysis than 100. But if you keep practicing past papers and have a good essay structure, then it is possible to score a distinction. PEA (Point, Example, Analysis) is a good way to write your essay. Also KIS (Keep it simple), you have to imagine that the examiner is a 10 year old and doesn't have much knowledge, so try to keep the essays simple and easy to read but make your points clear (edited)
@Romana 🇸🇰 also, PS3088 is a great choice if you will be taking Comparative Politics or Democracy and Democratisation. In both those subjects, the EU is pretty much mandatory to study and almost every time you get 1 exam question about the EU and sovereignty
