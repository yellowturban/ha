ilsatariq 15-Apr-20 08:03 PM
@Deleted User it really depends on what kind of a blog you want to start! If it’s on a particular subject like say, sports or makeup, then I guess the first step would be to spend some time acquiring a great deal of knowledge before producing your material on it. In my case, I simply put out my own writings that stem from my own personal experiences and thoughts— more like essays, narratives etc. So in that case you would be needing enormous will-power to be open and daring enough to do so as well as shunning the thought of feeling vulnerable and exposed. Because for me, writing is personal. It reflects the enclosed persona hidden within me. So before starting my blog, I had to come to terms with the fact that dozens of people would be peeking in my innermost thoughts. That was a challenge I had to overcome; just as how all writers ought to at some point.
2
Other than that, I really encourage you to start! Don’t think of perfecting things; that will come with time. Taking the step without thinking too much is what will push you to improve and excel I too, started spontaneously— without much thought and planning.
