@itachi I quite enjoyed Zankyou no Terror. The Story was really impactful, most of the plot was really well delivered upon (although I wasn't the biggest fan of the Showdown with Five). The themes that the anime covered and did so really well infact is pretty commendable. The way the story concluded managed to hit me. I think alot of people felt that impact in a similar way. One of the greater things about it for me was also the OST and Opening+Ending themes in general. Overall it was a really well made original animated series, definitely one of my favorite works By Mappa.
