9thStreetDreamer 🇺🇲 24-Apr-20 08:43 PM
Well from a legality stand point I would start by doing some research on the copy right and patent laws within your country and general region. See how well they are enforced and what the technical points are as pre requisites for you taking any kind of action. If you had a registered trademark or patent or some form of copy right protection on this idea or business concept then you can check to see how it can be enforced. Maybe speak to a legal advisor or look up law forums online asking about your issue and what the best way forward would be regarding it.
