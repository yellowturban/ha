Hello All, Here we will be posting all the relevant information that pertains to the societies feature of the server.  Lets begin with explaining the concept behind the societies and their general structure. I'll post this in a numbered question format for simplicity.
1- What are the societies? The societies are meant to act as a platform for our peers to collaborate, perform, act and express themselves on any particular idea regarding a certain interest. With the hope of allowing them to come interact with others who share in similar interests and ideas creating an opportunity to work together in different forms of activities.
5- If there is an activity/interest that you have which does not currently have a Society what should you do? Please feel free to share any ideas/suggestions that you may have regarding the creation of a society that does not currently exist which you are passionate about. You may message a Moderator or Admin directly or you may simply post about your suggestion in the 'Society Hub' Channel and mention a Moderator in your message. We will surely get back to you at the earliest possible.
Here is a list of societies currently on offer for the server: The Art Society The Music Society The Cinema Society The Photography Society The Culinary Society The Travel Society The Anime Society The Tech Enthusiasts Society The Memes Society The Environmental Society The Writing Society The Entrepreneurs Society The Sports Society This list will be updated as new society options are added.
